About this Site

Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provde the skier who really want to find fresh powder a way to find it.
Also allow people who are planning trips to see if they are going to run into fresh snow.
Overall just allow poeple to have a better experience while skiing both for causal and the hardcore skier.


Features:

Home:
- Allows you to search for the cities where ski resorts are located.
- Shows the current weather and snowfall amount.
- Shows a chart of the snowfall and rainfall every 3 hours for the next 5 days.
Region:
- Contains a list of the top ski resorts in North America based on PeakRankings.
- Broken into list of each region.
- Has the same function as the home where you can see the current weather and chart.
New Location:
- Allows you to add new resorts and gives you the snow report.
- Have to input the resort name, latitude, longitude.
Map:
- Show a map of the United States and the current snowfall movements.
